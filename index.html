About Us

What was the motivation behind the establishing of Emily Vision, and what does the name represent?

The motivation behind Emily Vision was to create a platform that empowers brands through creative and strategic communication. The name "Emily Vision" reflects the agency’s founder's vision and passion for transforming ideas into impactful stories that connect with audiences. It embodies a fresh, forward-thinking approach to PR and advertising.

When was this company founded, and what is its mission?

Emily Vision was founded in 2024 with the mission to deliver innovative and personalized PR and advertising strategies that help businesses thrive in a competitive market. Our aim is to be a trusted partner for companies looking to strengthen their brand presence and communicate more effectively with their target audience.

Why did we choose Cluj-Napoca as the location for Emily Vision?

Cluj-Napoca was chosen as the base for the company due to its dynamic business ecosystem, growing tech scene, and vibrant creative community. The city is a hub for innovation, making it the ideal location for an agency that values creativity, collaboration, and modern marketing practices.

What sets Emily Vision apart from other PR and advertising agencies?

The company stands out for its commitment to personalized service and a deep understanding of each client’s unique needs. We focus on building long-term relationships rather than just transactional services, ensuring that we create campaigns that truly reflect our clients' values and goals. Our agile approach allows us to quickly adapt to market trends and shifts in consumer behavior.

What are some of the core values that guide this company?

At Emily Vision, we are driven by creativity, integrity, and results. We believe in transparency and open communication with our clients, and we work hard to deliver measurable results that make a real difference. Our core values also include innovation, collaboration, and a commitment to staying at the forefront of industry trends.

What are Emily Vision’s goals for the next 5 years?

In the next five years, we aim to expand the reach both within Romania and internationally, helping more brands elevate their presence in the global market. We also plan to expand our service offerings and continue building a team of talented professionals. Ultimately, we aim to become a leading force in the PR and advertising space, known for our innovative campaigns and strategic insights.
